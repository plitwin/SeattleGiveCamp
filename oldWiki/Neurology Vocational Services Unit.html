Background Information

Neurology Vocational Services Unit "Neurology Vocational Services Unit is a nonprofit that provides a full range of employment services for individuals with neurological conditions, assisting them to lead satisfying and productive lives. In addition, through research, teaching and distribution of information, the unit acts as a national resource in the field of neurological vocation rehabilitation.

Our unit at Harborview is the only full-service program providing a full array of vocational rehabilitation services (evaluation, job assessments, job placement and job retention) to individuals with neurological condition in the Northwest. Each year, our team provides these services to more than 300 individuals with multiple sclerosis, epilepsy, traumatic brain injury, stroke and other neurological condition.
