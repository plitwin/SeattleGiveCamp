Project Proposal


HSDC has developed a new ENews template in Vertical Response, which is their online service provider for email and direct mail (postcard) campaigns. With in VR there are premade templates and the option for a canvas to design your their templates using HTML. Staff chose a design and did some slight modifications to it but are not happy with the look - it looks cluttered and not as crisp and clean as they would like. HSDC would like to improve the look and navigation of the newsletter template - so a graphic designer for this type of publication and someone who knows HTML would be great!


They are upgrading to a new web site and would like these 2 pieces of communication to look related. They can supply some copies of screen shots of the new web site under construction. They also have an idea of what they would like the template to look like.
