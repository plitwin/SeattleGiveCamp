Project Proposal

They want a google maps page that shows their alumni. The whole thing can be done in Salesforce.
The information is stored in the Account table.

Because they have more than 200 alumni we will need to aggregate data based on the zoom of the map. So if they zoom out to the entire world we should not try to give Google Maps 1000 records. Rather aggregate by country. When the zoom level becomes appropriate we should show the individual information (TBD see note).
