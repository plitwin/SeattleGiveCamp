Project Proposal

Friends of Youth is seeking creative strategies to better communicate with our clients, homeless youth and young adults in east King County. We envision using both social media and cell phones for this dialogue and would welcome creative solutions. Most of our clients have phones but not all of them have smart phones. We would like to have an avenue to communicate important information like a shelter closure or the estimated arrival time of our Outreach van in an Eastside community, or to announce the availability of donated clothing for a job hunt or similar information in real time.
