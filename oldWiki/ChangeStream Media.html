Mission and History

ChangeStream Media is a Seattle-based, volunteer-powered nonprofit that promotes education, health and social justice worldwide through digital storytelling. Their productions include stop-motion animation, documentaries, scripted dramas, puppet shows and music videos. They also teach video production workshops for Seattle-area nonprofits, empowering them to advance social causes through digital storytelling.

Project Proposal


Our website uses Wordpress running the free Woo theme "Irresistible." Our most basic need is for an "Annual Checkup" to make sure the website is running well. At last year's GiveCamp we learned about a number of plugins to provide backups, prevent hacking, blocking comment spam, and more that never would have crossed our minds. We would love some fresh eyes to see that we are using the best add-ons for our basic needs like volunteer forms, a content slider that keeps malfunctioning, and feeds from our social media platforms. With one or two Wordpress knowledgable folks I'm sure we could greatly improve the functionality of our site.
If we had additional volunteers or specialists available we have a few more ambitious projects we would love to work on that still could be done in a weekend:
  • We are currently editing an ebook on nonprofit video production. We would love assistance in learning how to add multimedia content to our InDesign document if someone had that expertise.
  • We would be very open to any graphic design ideas that would freshen up the look of the site.
  • Advice on migrating to a new theme. Last year we learned how old, unsupported themes can develop security issues - we are hoping ours is OK, but if not we might need to shift the focus to this task.
  • If someone wants a real project, we have some ideas for tools for crowdsourcing video that could be really useful in the nonprofit arena, but we have no idea how technically difficult they would be to build
