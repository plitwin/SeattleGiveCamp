Project Proposal

"We would like to create a mobile app version of our Core Values exercise, possibly one that could be linked to Twitter or Facebook. With the Core Values exercise, participants consider their core values, and focus them down to three specific values that they would like to carry with them in their work and personal lives. We believe that one of the first steps of Ethical Leadership is knowing and claiming your core values, and this simple exercise can be done individually or with a group and is a great way in to larger conversations about values and leadership.

We would like the app to offer an easy way to go through all of the steps of the exercise, and provide a way to give the participant an opportunity to add his/her own values.
The full pdf with detailed directions can be downloaded from our website: http://ethicalleadership.org/publications/free-resources-1.
Thank you for considering our request. We look forward to hearing from you soon."
