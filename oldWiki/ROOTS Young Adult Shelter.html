Project:

The most important tools that are used in day-to-day management of our shelter are the Individual Guest Sign In binders. These are used every evening to track the guests who access our shelter and to manage communication with guests. To best facilitate our growth and the increased number of guests who use our facility, our goal is to fully move our Guest Sign In process from hand written, to a digital system.

We are looking for a tool to digitize the Sign In process and that will allow us to automatically generate reports.

In addition, we’d like this tool to:
  • Show popup reminders and messages that are auto-generated by date
  • Require certain fields to be filled in
  • Generate Daily Reports that include who stayed on that specific night
  • Ability to enter messages that will pop up when the person signs in
  • Program is password protected and secure


About:

ROOTS Young Adult Shelter provides emergency shelter for young adults, ages 18-25. In addition to 45 beds available seven days a week, we also offer our guests hot meals, showers, laundry, and opportunities to work with case managers. More information about our organization is online at www.rootsinfo.org.

Since December 2012, our shelter has increased capacity from 25 to 45 beds. This expansion has been a huge milestone in our growth as an organization and our ability to impact homelessness in Seattle. At the same time, nearly doubling our shelter capacity has led to challenges in daily operations – especially in regard to completing the nightly Sign In process quickly and accurately.
