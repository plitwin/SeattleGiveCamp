About

DISCOVERU is a non-profit prevention organization that is dedicated to providing a powerful inside out solution to problems that today’s youth face every day. These challenges have grown to epidemic proportions and need immediate interventions. We believe that:

Wellness is the BEST Prevention

DiscoverU helps youth and families understand that “State of Mind” is one of the primary key foundational human factors that resides at the core of our wellness, innate health and wisdom. Once kids understand how they function in their natural wisdom and innate health, they can access it more naturally and effortlessly creating the resiliency that allows them to overcome anything that life throws at them.

Our workshops help youth and families to put to rest the self-doubt, frustration, chronic negative emotions, confusion, anxiety and the stress that often leads to bad decisions and behaviors that tears apart lives.
